## 摘要
强化学习（RL）已成为大语言模型（LLMs）后训练的核心范式，特别是在复杂推理任务中，但它经常面临探索崩溃（exploration collapse）的问题：策略过早地集中在一小部分主导推理模式上，虽然提高了 pass@1，但限制了 rollout 层面的多样性以及 pass@k 的提升。我们认为，这种失败源于对局部 token 行为的正则化，而非对解集多样性的正则化。为了解决这一问题，我们提出了唯一性感知强化学习（Uniqueness-Aware Reinforcement Learning），这是一种 rollout 层面的目标函数，显式地奖励那些展现出稀有高层策略的正确解。我们的方法使用基于 LLM 的裁判，根据高层解题策略对同一问题的多个 rollout 进行聚类，忽略表面的差异，并根据聚类大小的反比重新加权策略优势（policy advantages）。因此，正确且新颖的策略比冗余的策略获得更高的奖励。在数学、物理和医学推理基准测试中，我们的方法在大采样预算下持续提高了 pass@k，并在不牺牲 pass@1 的情况下增加了 pass@k 曲线下的面积（AUC@K），同时在大规模应用中维持了探索并发现了更多样化的解题策略。

## Abstract
Reinforcement learning (RL) has become a central paradigm for post-training large language models (LLMs), particularly for complex reasoning tasks, yet it often suffers from exploration collapse: policies prematurely concentrate on a small set of dominant reasoning patterns, improving pass@1 while limiting rollout-level diversity and gains in pass@k. We argue that this failure stems from regularizing local token behavior rather than diversity over sets of solutions. To address this, we propose Uniqueness-Aware Reinforcement Learning, a rollout-level objective that explicitly rewards correct solutions that exhibit rare high-level strategies. Our method uses an LLM-based judge to cluster rollouts for the same problem according to their high-level solution strategies, ignoring superficial variations, and reweights policy advantages inversely with cluster size. As a result, correct but novel strategies receive higher rewards than redundant ones. Across mathematics, physics, and medical reasoning benchmarks, our approach consistently improves pass@$k$ across large sampling budgets and increases the area under the pass@$k$ curve (AUC@$K$) without sacrificing pass@1, while sustaining exploration and uncovering more diverse solution strategies at scale.
